For the Self Who Survived

Some things are not meant to be explained. Only felt.

You don’t have to come back. I’ll come to you. I won’t yank on the cord or demand your return. I will simply send Divine love to wherever you are. If you’re hiding behind a memory— I’ll lay soft grass outside the door. If you’re crouched in a moment that never stopped hurting— I’ll open the sky and let the light come slowly. I don’t need you to heal to be worthy. You already are. When you’re ready— if you’re ever ready— the field will feel different. The air will feel kinder. The wind won’t chase you. It’ll ask you to play. Until then, I’ll be here. Not waiting. Holding space. And if you ever feel a warmth for no reason at all— that’s me. Loving you through time.
You can come in. Take your time.
